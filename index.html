I’m an assistant professor (tenured) at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam in Amsterdam, Netherlands, and my research focusses on the performance and security aspects of networked systems. Previously, I was a senior researcher at the Max-Planck-Institut für Informatik in Saarbrücken, Germany. I graduated with a PhD in 2016 from Duke University in Durham, USA and was advised by Prof. Bruce MacDowell Maggs.
